## Mobile Device Management (MDM)

To manage all of the mobile phones used in the organaization we need to use MDM (Mobile Device Management).

• This role is done by a Sys admin
• Who used to set policies and Configure the functionality of things like camera or GPS ect..
• MDM is the process of making the separate partition in the mobile device

• For this there are some of the options to use mobile device like

**BYOD (Bring your own device) also called as Bring your own technology.**

**COPE (Corporate owned, personally enabled)**

**CYOD (Choose your own device)**

• MDM console is the app to control all the mobile devices in the company
• It also use for the synchronizing.
• It is used for manage things like Email, Cloud storage and other services.
