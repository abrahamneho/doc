### Common Wireless Tech
**WIFI Network**
- which is standardized by IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers)
- They handles 802.11 Committee
- Network with version
  - [ ] 802.11ac (WI-FI 5)
  - [ ] 802.11ax (WI-FI 6) & (WI-FI 6E) for extended
  - [ ] 802.11be (WI-FI 7)communication
- Different frequencies in WIFI Networks
  - [ ] 2.4 GHz > it has 20 MHz
  - [ ] 5 GHz > it has 20 MHz > 40 MHz > 80 MHz > 160 MHz
  - [ ] 6 GHz > it has 20 MHz > 40 MHz > 80 MHz > 160 MHz > 320 MHz

**Bluetooth**
- It use 2.4 GHz range
- It is referred to as the unlicensed ISM (Industrial, Scientific, Medical Band)
- It has short range like 10 meters

**RFID (Radio frequency idrntification)**
- It is used in Badges, inside of our pets, it used to scam and know the details
- It has no battery in RFID tag, know we bring a scanner close to the device, it will power from that radio frequency but some has battery which want to be actively powered.

**NFC (Near Field Communication)**
- It is build on RFID Tech
- Popular use of NFC is to use your phone or your smartwatch for the payment
- It can used to open the door, ect..
